About Us

The largest school

Dhambojhi Secondary School, Nepalganj (धम्बोजी माध्यमिक विद्यालय, नेपालगंज) was established in 1957 AD (2014 B.S) as a community based educational institution, which is situated in Nepalgunj-1, Dhambojhi, Waterpark, Nepalgunj, Banke, Nepal. It is affiliated to the National Examination Board (NEB) and approved by the Ministry of Education. It offers educational programs from ECD to grade 10 and Plus Two (Class 11 and 12) in Management, Science, Computer Engineering, and Education streams. Dhambojhi Secondary School, Nepalgunj, Banke provides Ten Plus Two level education with affordable fees structures. It also offers scholarships facilities for deserving, hard-working, and poor students. All the activities of this school are a keen focus on excellent education and committed to maintain academic quality and to ensure achievement.
